College Ministry

Here at MVCC, we firmly believe college students are a part of the next generation that will change the world.

EMERGE is our college ministry that anchors on three words: "Reach, Raise, and Relay." We believe in reaching people with the story of Jesus, a story that has changed us and continues to change the world around us. We believe in raising up people to be life-long and devoted followers of Jesus that will live his story authentically. Finally, we believe in relaying people back to the places God has called them to; campus, work, community, etc...) in order that they may share the life and the story they follow.

EMERGE meets on Thursdays at 7:30pm. Follow us on our social media platforms to stay up to date with our semester schedules, mission trip opportunities, retreats, and special events.
